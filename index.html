A Half-Meme Website for the Beloved City From Fortnite: Battle Royale

A Summer Project By Matt Ngaw

What is Tilted Towers?

Tilted Towers is a dense urban area, located near the west of the center of the map, adjacent to Loot Lake and Shifty Shafts. Close-combat with skilled players is almost guaranteed, as a popular method of racking up kills is to land in Tilted Towers.

How Good is Tilted Towers?

There are 15 buildings, all of which contain loot. There can be up to 37 chests in this region of the map, along with many areas to collect materials, especially wood. Leaving Tilted Towers victorious is difficult. However, it provides the victor with the best gear and weapons for the rest of the game.
